# SBI ELSS Tax Saver Fund (Formerly known as SBI Long Term Equity Fund)
> Official SBI Mutual Fund ELSS tax-saving equity scheme.

## Fund Overview
SBI ELSS Tax Saver Fund is an equity linked savings scheme designed as a tax saving tool under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act, 1961. The fund aims to provide investors with long-term capital appreciation by investing in a portfolio of equity shares. It invests a minimum of 80% of its assets in equity and related instruments. Investments are subject to a statutory lock-in period of 3 years.

## Taxation
To deliver the benefit of investment in a portfolio of equity shares, while offering deduction on such investment made in the scheme under Section 80C of the Income-tax Act, 1961. Investments in this scheme are subject to a statutory lock-in of 3 years from the date of allotment to avail Section 80C benefits.
