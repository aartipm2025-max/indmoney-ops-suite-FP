# SBI Midcap Fund
> Official SBI Mutual Fund mid cap equity scheme.

## Fund Overview
SBI Midcap Fund aims to provide investors with opportunities for long-term growth in capital by investing predominantly in a well-diversified basket of equity stocks of midcap companies. The fund predominantly invests (minimum 65%) in midcap stocks. SBI Midcap Fund selectively invests the remaining assets in other equities and debt instruments. The fund follows a blend of growth and value style with a bottom-up stock selection strategy.
