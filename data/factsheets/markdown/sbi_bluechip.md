# SBI Large Cap Fund (Formerly known as SBI Bluechip Fund)
> Official SBI Mutual Fund large cap equity scheme.

## Fund Overview
To provide investors with opportunities for long-term growth in capital through an active management of investments in a diversified basket of large cap equity stocks. The fund invests predominantly in large cap companies as specified by SEBI/AMFI.

## Key Facts
- Category: Equity
- Sub-category: Large Cap
- Benchmark: BSE 100 TRI (Additional Benchmark: BSE Sensex TRI)
- Fund Manager: Mr. Saurabh Pant
- Inception Date: 14 Feb 2006
- AUM (₹ Cr): 48,925.75 (as of 31 Mar 2026)
- NAV (Direct Growth): ₹93.2630 (as of 31 Mar 2026)
- Risk Rating: Very High

## Expense Ratio
- Regular plan: 1.52%
- Direct plan: 0.84%

## Exit Load
- For exit within 30 days from the date of allotment — 0.25%
- For exit after 30 days and within 90 days from the date of allotment — 0.10%
- For exit after 90 days from the date of allotment — Nil

## Minimum Investment
- SIP: ₹500
- Lumpsum: ₹5,000
- Additional purchase: ₹1,000
