# SBI Equity Hybrid Fund
> Official SBI Mutual Fund aggressive hybrid scheme.

## Fund Overview
SBI Equity Hybrid Fund aims to provide investors with opportunities for long-term capital appreciation with liquidity of an open-ended scheme by investing in a mix of debt and equity. The fund invests in a diversified portfolio of stocks of high growth companies and balances risk through fixed income investments. It invests minimum 65% in equity and allocates 20-35% to debt and money market instruments.
